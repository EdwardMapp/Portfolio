WALL TAKEDOWN



This animation was also created with Motion Capture. I worked on this short test with a classmate's performance, utilizing a gym mat to simulate the wall that is animated to be taken down in the short. The perfomance was done on Motive and brought to MotionBuilder for retargeting, and the camerawork and sequencing was done on Unreal Engine.



3D BEDROOM MODEL RENDER



On Maya, I modeled this bedroom based on a reference I found online. For the assignment, I needed to arrange lights and provide a high quality render of the model. With additional work with textures, this is something I imagine could possibly be used in a 3D animation, whether it'd be for a movie, show, or video game.
