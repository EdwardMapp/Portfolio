THE UNSEEN



Using my university's building, a camera, and myself, I managed to create a horror short where I'm escaping from a monster. The primary limitation in this project was the fact that I didn't have the budget or resources to create a fictional looking monster for me to escape from. To work around this, the monster's presence is implied through the acting and sound design even though it cannot be seen onscreen. The post-production video editing was on on Premiere Pro and the sound design was worked on in Reaper and Premiere Pro.



VIDEO EFFECTS EDITING



With footage I've taken of the city, I applied video effects from Premiere Pro to provided a distorted experience to the viewer. The goal of the editing was to experiment the ways in which I can convey fatigue, sickness, and nausea through camera techniques and editing techniques.



VIDEO MONTAGE


While not having a strict narrative, the video montage captures various parts of the city while showing myself in some of these areas, or thinking about them in my own room. It experiments with the ways color grading can be utilized to make different settings look distinct and present different moods.
